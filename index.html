Who we are

The Official Astronomy Club of October STEM School

As a student-run club affiliated with STEM High School for Boys – 6th of October, we provide physics training, host competitions, and publish academic materials for high schoolers from all around the world.

Specializations

Our Branches

Dive deep into a specific area of astronomical science with one of our specialized sub-groups.

01

Observational Astronomy

Hands-on telescope work, planet tracking, and lunar surface mapping sessions for all experience levels.

02

Celestial Mechanics

Orbital dynamics, eclipses, and the mathematical models behind planetary and lunar motion.

03

Astrophotography

Camera setups, long-exposure techniques, and image processing for stunning night-sky photography.

04

Islamic Astronomy

Crescent moon sighting, the Islamic lunar calendar, and the rich heritage of Arab astronomers.
